This styling meraki an fiberous wax with a super strong hold and a volume booster.It can even be used as a pre-styler

The lightweight formula keeps your hair feeling light with a volume boost without compromising on it's strong hold.

It has a beeswax,plant wax based formula.Paraben and sulphate free which means there is no harsh chemcials that will harm your hair. It has a subtle mint fragrance, with a fiberous texture.

It is versatile for any hair type whether is it thick or thin, it will always keep your hair light. Can be used as a pre-styler and a post-styler. As a pre-styler it helps to style your hair and prevent heat damage. As a post-styler it provides a super strong hold.

Directions:

Apply a small amount into damp hair and blow-dry into desired direction. After blow-drying apply small amount into hair to lock it into place.
