Details

ByVilain SideKick is a pre-styler meant for blowdrying. It is a heat protectant which protects your hair from heat damage, it also gives a medium hold to your hair and adding volume to your hair at the same time. Maintaing a healthy looking hair.

It can also be used after styling as a hair spray which will give your hair an even stronger hold and give it a natural shine.

Directions:

Spray on damp hair before blow-drying. Then blow-dry with a comb or fingers to desired look. Use any clay or wax to style blow-dried hair. Finish by spraying SideKick into your hair from a distance.
