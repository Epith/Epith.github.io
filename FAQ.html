FAQ

  • Do you ship internationally?
  • Yes, we frequently ship internationally...But only to United States, United Kingdom and Singapore. Please be aware that the shipping delivery times for international orders may vary from 3 to 6 weeks depending on the country for the most economical option. We have a better option for international shipments, but it is more costly.
  • How much is shipping?
  • Singapore:
    Shipping is free if you go to the pick-up point to collect your item. However there will be a shipping cost of SGD$3.50 for registered mail, or SGD$5 for express delivery.

    United States & United Kingdom:
    Flat shipping fee of SGD$15.00.
  • Do you offer cash on delivery?
  • Yes, we do. However, Cash on Delivery is only available for meet ups.
  • Will my items be damaged via normal mail?
  • We ship out orders in bubble wrap and high quality water resistant envelopes, handling each package with care. However, if the items are damaged upon delivery, just contact us and we will provide an one for one exchange.
  • Where is the meet up location?
  • Meet up will be at Bishan MRT Station.
  • Can I cancel my order?
  • You can cancel your order before making any payments. However once a payment is made we can't assure you that your order will be cancelled due to complications.

About Us

Founded in 2016. HairStop is a small group of polytechnic students with the aim of providing a variety of hair products to the everyday man, giving them tips to style their hair with hopes of making them a better version of themselves.
